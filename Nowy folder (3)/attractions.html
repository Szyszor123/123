Attractions of the Region

Teutonic Castle in Człuchów

The Teutonic castle located in Człuchów is the second largest castle of the Order after Malbork. It was the most difficult object in Pomerania to conquer, the seat of the commander and the Teutonic convent. Ulrich von Jungingen and Konrad von Wallenrode resided there. Currently, the castle houses a Regional Museum with permanent and temporary exhibitions and a library. The events and historical performances organized there are very popular among residents and visiting guests. The castle is surrounded by a beautiful English park - Luiza's Forest.

Water ski and wakeboard lift in Szczecinek

One of the longest ski lifts in Europe located on Lake Trzesiecko. Situated in the city center surrounded by a historic city park filled with greenery, it makes... it is visited by water skiers and wakeboarders not only for the sports attractions, but also for the unique atmosphere of the place. The park stretches along the entire lake and has numerous squares and active recreation zones. For romantics and rose fans - a rose garden. In addition to renting water equipment, the city also offers city bikes.

Zoo Canpol

A zoo hidden in the forest in prepared areas consistent with the natural life of animals with an area of ​​4,870 m2. The garden is inhabited by 39 species of animals, about 100 inhabitants, and the most valuable species are the Siberian Tiger, the Pygmy Hippopotamus, Red kangaroo, African ostrich, Chapman's zebra. Canpol also invites you to Eurobungee, a go-kart track and Paddle Boat - water fun for the youngest.

Insect Park in Lubnica

Can you hide under an ant? How about hugging a bee? Now it's possible. We invite you to the one and only Insect Park in Lubnica. In the Park you can find realistic models of insects, made in gigantic magnification. These sculptures are made with precise accuracy and attention to their morphological structure. Gigantic ones allow us to better understand the structure of these individuals. A walk along the educational and nature path is an excellent educational material for every age group and a great biology lesson. Only here you will encounter a giant fly, a grasshopper or a spider. The exhibition of figures was enriched by an exhibition of live insects from... various corners of our globe.

Przytoń Nature Reserve

One of the youngest landscape nature reserves in our area, but also particularly appreciated by tourists. Here, on an area of ​​20 ha, there is a huge forest of beautiful beeches growing on the escarpment of the Brda River. Most tourists admire the charms of the reserve from the level of kayaks. The greatest attractions lie in the upper part of the forest - beeches and pines, which are very impressive - they are over 200 years old. Huge tree trunks that are dying are not managed by humans. It is on them that new trees grow, various types of fungi develop, and animals that depend on decaying plants develop. In addition to the natural values, you can see aesthetic values ​​here that will be unforgettable for people who live in the city.

Canpol Racing Karting Track in Człuchów

The Canpol Racing karting track in Człuchów is one of the most modern amateur karting tracks in Poland. The 600-meter-long track is equipped with a two-sector F1-style time measurement, and the results are displayed live during the ride on an impressively sized LED board. Professionally installed barriers ensure maximum safety, the surrounding greenery pleases the eye while driving, and artificial lighting ensures great visibility even after dark.

Rope Park in Człuchów

The Rope Park in Człuchów is great fun for children and adults. Active recreation, adventure and a great adrenaline rush available to everyone, regardless of age or physical fitness. Overcome your weaknesses and take advantage of our attractions "in such beautiful natural surroundings." The rope park consists of 3 separate stages with varying degrees of difficulty: a children's route (16 obstacles), a medium route (19 obstacles) and a difficult route (27 obstacles).

Water Park in Człuchów

It is an outdoor facility, operating seasonally. The Water Park has slides in the form of a gutter, a pipe and a multi-lane slide. There is also a children's pool with small slides (elephant slides, multi-lane family slides) and a small wild river.
